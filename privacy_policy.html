Privacy Policy for Frog Sort Fun

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 10X Digital Studio ("we", "us", "our"), a developer based in Bangladesh, handles information in connection with the mobile game Frog Sort Fun (the "App"). By downloading or using the App, you agree to this Policy. The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

1. Information We and Our Partners Process

The App itself does not ask you for, or collect, personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera. Your gameplay progress (levels, coins, settings) is stored only locally on your device.

However, third-party services integrated in the App may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID) and ad interaction data, used to show and measure ads (Google AdMob).
  • Device and usage information such as device model, operating system version, language, country, app version, a non-resettable or resettable app instance identifier, and general (non-precise) location derived from IP address, used for analytics and crash diagnostics (Google Firebase / Analytics).

2. How This Information Is Used

  • To display and measure advertisements, including limiting how often ads appear.
  • To understand how the App is used and to improve gameplay and stability.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following service providers, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — How Google uses information from ads and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase / Google Analytics for Firebase (analytics) — Firebase Privacy and Security.
  • Google Play Services — Google Privacy Policy.

4. Advertising and Your Choices

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized. You can opt out of ad personalization or reset your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads on Android). Where required by law, we (through Google's consent tools) ask for your consent before showing personalized ads.

5. Users in the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

If you are in these regions, our legal bases for processing are your consent (for personalized advertising and analytics) and our legitimate interests (to operate, secure, and improve the App). You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, to object to processing, to data portability, and to withdraw consent at any time. Before serving personalized ads, the App presents a Google-certified consent message (UMP). You may change your choice later from the App's settings or your device settings.

6. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not "sell" personal information for money. Certain ad-related data sharing may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under California law; you may opt out of personalized advertising as described in Section 4. California residents have the right to know, delete, and correct personal information, and not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. We follow Google Play's Families policy and configure our ad partners accordingly. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. Information processed by our service providers is retained according to their policies. We and our providers use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
