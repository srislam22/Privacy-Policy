Privacy Policy

App: Lumi: Glow Puzzle · Developer: 10X Digital Studio · Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumi: Glow Puzzle ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the game and show ads.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not ask for or store your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or any account login. Your game progress (unlocked levels and stars) is saved only on your own device.

Information Collected Automatically by Third Parties

We use the following third-party services that may collect limited information to provide ads and understand app usage:

  • Google AdMob (advertising). AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier (Advertising ID), IP address, and general device/app information to serve and measure ads. See Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and AdMob partners: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903.
  • Google Firebase / Google Analytics for Firebase (anonymous, aggregated usage analytics such as level starts and completions, app version, and device type). See: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Advertising ID

The App uses the Android Advertising ID for ads. You can reset or delete it, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings under Settings > Privacy > Ads.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are configured to be non-personalized where required. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will remove it.

Your Choices (GDPR / EEA & UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you will be shown a consent prompt for personalized ads where required, and you may withdraw consent at any time via the in-app settings or your device ad settings. You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data processed by our ad/analytics partners as described in their policies above.

Your Choices (California / CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to opt out of its "sale" or "sharing". The App does not sell personal information. Ad personalization can be limited through your device settings.

Data Retention & Security

On-device progress remains until you delete the App or clear its data. Data handled by Google AdMob and Firebase is retained per Google's policies. We rely on industry-standard safeguards provided by these services; data is transmitted over encrypted connections.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
